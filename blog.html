What are the defferences Between Var,Let & Constant

The scope of a var variable is functional scope, the scope of a let variable is block scope, the scope of a const variable is block scope. Var can be updated and redeclared in scope, Let can be updated but not redeclared in scope, Constant cannot be updated or redeclared in scope.

What are the defferences Between Arrow Functions & Regular Functions?

Arrow functions Unlike regular functions, arrow functions do not have (this). Its value inside an arrow function remains the same throughout the function's lifecycle and is always bound to its value in the nearest non-arrow parent function.
